A Letter to Lokkhi

My Dearest Lokkhi,

I still smile when I think about how it all started. On 25th May 2025, you sent me a friend request. The next day, 26th May, I texted you first — just a simple “Hey.” And your first reply was, “Are you real or fake?” That one line started everything… who knew such a funny beginning would turn into something so beautiful?

From that day, you became more than just a name on my screen. You became my mentor, my guide, and slowly, my heart’s favorite person. Then on 17th June, I finally said what I’d been feeling — that I love you.

Now when I look back, it all feels like fate. You came into my life so unexpectedly, but now I can’t imagine my days without you. Thank you for being my Lokkhi — kind, smart, and mine.

Always yours,
Swapneel
